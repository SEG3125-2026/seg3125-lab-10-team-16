**Deadline:** April 5, 2026 11:59 PM · **Weight:** 3% · **Video:** **under 10 minutes**

## Video must show (per slides)

1. **Greeting / welcome** – start of conversation, clear intro  
2. **Booking request** – user books a service  
3. **Partial-information handling** – user gives several slots at once; bot only asks for **missing** fields  
4. **Missing fields** – explicit prompt for what CX still needs  
5. **Confirmation step** – full summary + user confirms  
6. **One fallback / error case** – nonsense input or invalid request  
7. **Console footage:** your **flows/pages** in Dialogflow CX  
8. **Console footage:** **parameters** and **entities** (screens or quick scroll)
