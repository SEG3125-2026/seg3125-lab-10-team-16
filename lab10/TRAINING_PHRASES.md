# Training phrases (examples for intents)

Add **10–20 variations** per intent where possible. Mix politeness, slang, short vs long.

## Start / welcome (Default Start Flow)

- Hi  
- Hello  
- I need a haircut  
- Book an appointment  
- What can you do?  
- Hey there  

## Booking – start

- I want to book a colour tomorrow  
- Can I get a men’s cut with Michael next Friday?  
- Reserve women’s haircut Saturday 2pm with Emma  
- Appointment for beard trim please  
- Haircut and beard combo any time Thursday  

## Booking – partial (for demo)

- **Women’s haircut with Sarah tomorrow at 3** (service + stylist + date + time — bot asks only contact)  
- **Men’s cut at 10 am Monday** (missing stylist → ask)  
- **Colour with James** (missing date/time → ask)  

## Support – services

- What services do you offer?  
- How much is a beard trim?  
- Do you do colour?  
- Prices for haircut  

## Support – hours / location

- What are your hours?  
- Are you open Saturday?  
- Where is Elite Cuts?  
- Address please  

## Confirmation

- Yes  
- Yep confirm it  
- Sounds good  
- No cancel that  
- Change the time  

## Fallback test (record in video)

- **Asdfgh blah blah**  
- **I want a spaceship haircut on Mars**  

Expect polite clarification + menu options.
